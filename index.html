Product Portfolio

Real World Data Pipeline (B2B)

Developed an ETL pipeline at Freenome, taking it from concept to launch. The pipeline processed RWD from diverse sources and formats, standardizing it into a single Common Data Model (CDM). This unified data powered machine learning models and analyses for early cancer detection. The product served internal research teams and external partners by normalizing raw data received from them and returning it in a standardized format, while retaining the data for internal research purposes.

Analysis Pipeline (B2B)

Built AI-powered diagnostic software for Freenome's flagship blood test kit product, designed to assess colorectal cancer (CRC) risk. Once patient samples were received, they were processed in the lab and analyzed through this AI pipeline. Machine learning models evaluated the data to determine a risk score, enabling early identification of patients at risk for CRC.

Vintus Ordering Web Product (B2C)

Managed the development and optimization of a customer-facing web product for order management. Collaborated with development, sales, and marketing teams to design a user-friendly portal where customers could place orders, track order statuses, and access detailed product information.

AI Chatbot Mobile App (B2C)

Led feature prioritization, user research, and design decisions for the AskAetna Student Health App, a widely used healthcare chatbot mobile app. Focused on enhancing user experience and delivering meaningful features based on user feedback and market needs.

Certification Tracking Database (Internal)

Designed and launched a Certification Tracking Database App from the ground up, streamlining the internal certification submission process. This tool reduced project staffing times by enabling managers to quickly access a centralized database of employees with specific training or certifications, optimizing resource allocation and team assembly.

Responsible for end-to-end development, product management, and design of this innovative solution.
